# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is my data safe?

Yes, though we recommend to join our discord server for annoucements about our servers.

## Can other people access my files?

No, your servers are isolated in **docker containers** which are secure and fast

## What are the networking speeds?

All of our servers run at 1gbps network.

## What are eggs

Eggs are the premade servers types, which you can deploy

## What are nodes?

Nodes are the servers which host your containers.
